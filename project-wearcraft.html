About the Project

WearCraft Digital is an innovative AI-powered clothing customization platform that revolutionizes the fashion design experience. Users can design custom outfits with interactive avatars, choosing from various fabrics, colors, patterns, and embellishments.

The platform leverages artificial intelligence to suggest similar styles and integrates Google Images for design inspiration, making it a comprehensive solution for personalized fashion.
